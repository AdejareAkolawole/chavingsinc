Job Description

We are looking for an experienced web developer to help us build a modern e-commerce platform. The ideal candidate will have strong skills in front-end and back-end development, with expertise in React and Node.js.

Our company specializes in selling handmade crafts and we need a platform that can showcase our products beautifully while providing a seamless shopping experience for our customers.

The successful candidate will work closely with our design team to implement the UI/UX designs and ensure that the platform is responsive, fast, and accessible.

Requirements

  • At least 3 years of experience in web development
  • Strong proficiency in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript
  • Experience with React.js and Node.js
  • Familiarity with MongoDB or similar NoSQL databases
  • Understanding of RESTful APIs and modern web protocols
  • Knowledge of responsive design and cross-browser compatibility
  • Experience with e-commerce platforms is a plus
  • Ability to work independently and meet deadlines
  • Strong communication skills and attention to detail

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain our e-commerce platform using React.js and Node.js
  • Implement responsive designs and ensure cross-browser compatibility
  • Integrate with payment gateways and shipping APIs
  • Optimize application for maximum speed and scalability
  • Collaborate with designers to implement UI/UX features
  • Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs
  • Maintain code quality, organization, and automatization
  • Troubleshoot and debug issues as they arise

Application Process

To apply for this position, please submit your resume, portfolio, and a cover letter explaining why you are a good fit for this role. We also encourage you to share examples of your previous work, especially any e-commerce projects you have worked on.

The selection process will involve:

  1. Initial resume screening
  2. Technical interview
  3. Practical coding test
  4. Final interview with the team

We look forward to hearing from you!
